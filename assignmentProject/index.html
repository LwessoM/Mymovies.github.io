My Favorite Films of all Time

by Lwesso Mukeni

  • Joker
  • The Pursuit of Happyness
  • The Dark Knight
  • Scarface
  • The Wolf of Wall Street

1. Joker

Year: 2019 | Rating: R

Joaquin Phoenix delivers a chilling performance in this psychological thriller about a failed comedian who becomes Gotham's infamous villain, the Joker.

2. The Pursuit of Happyness

Year: 2006 | Rating: PG-13

A heartfelt story of struggle and perseverance, starring Will Smith as a father who fights to create a better life for his son.

3. The Dark Knight

Year: 2008 | Rating: PG-13

Christopher Nolan's masterpiece pits Batman against his greatest adversary, the Joker, played in an iconic performance by Heath Ledger.

4. Scarface

Year: 1983 | Rating: R

Al Pacino delivers a legendary performance as Tony Montana, a Cuban immigrant who rises to the top of the Miami drug scene.

5. The Wolf of Wall Street

Year: 2013 | Rating: R

Martin Scorsese's wild tale of Wall Street excess, starring Leonardo DiCaprio as Jordan Belfort, a man who rose to great wealth through dubious means.
